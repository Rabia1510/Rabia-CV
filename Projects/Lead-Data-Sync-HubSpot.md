#Google Sheets to HubSpot Lead Synchronization
​# Project Overview
​This project automates the flow of lead data from external sources (Google Sheets) into HubSpot CRM. It ensures that every new lead is captured instantly, preventing data entry errors and providing real-time performance analytics for marketing teams.
​# Tools Used
​Zapier: Workflow Automation logic.
​Google Sheets: Source of lead data.
​HubSpot CRM: Centralized Lead Management & Reporting.
​Data Analytics: HubSpot Dashboards for conversion tracking.
​# How it Works
​Trigger: A new row is added to a specific Google Sheet (e.g., from an Etsy export or manual lead list).
​Action: Zapier fetches the data and performs a "Create or Update Contact" action in HubSpot.
​Data Enrichment: The system checks if the contact exists to avoid duplicates (Deduplication).
​Outcome: Leads are immediately available for the sales team, and performance metrics (Emails sent, Open rates, Clicks) are tracked in the HubSpot Marketing Dashboard.
​✅ Key Achievements
​Real-time Sync: Eliminated the delay between lead capture and CRM entry.
​Reporting Readiness: Enabled marketing analytics to track email engagement (50% Click Rate achieved in test samples).
​Process Efficiency: 100% automation of the data migration pipeline.
