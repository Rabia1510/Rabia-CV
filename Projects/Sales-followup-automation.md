# 🚀 Multi-Stage Sales Follow-Up Automation

An advanced, multi-stage sales follow-up automation blueprint built on *Make.com. This system consolidates multiple operational scenarios into a unified, high-converting pipeline designed to nurture leads, log data, and trigger timely follow-ups inside **ClickUp* without manual intervention.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
Manual lead follow-up is prone to delays and missed opportunities. This master automation blueprint acts as a central engine that captures incoming leads, evaluates their stage, triggers automated email sequences, and updates team communication channels instantly. 

* *Goal:* Maximize lead conversion rate by ensuring 100% consistent, timely touchpoints.
* *Architecture:* Multi-stage scenario consolidation for seamless data routing and reduced operation usage.

---

## 🛠️ Apps Connected
* *Webhooks* (Instant lead capture)
* *ClickUp* (Lead logging, task updates, and pipeline tracking)
* *Gmail / SMTP* (Automated personalized follow-up emails)
* *Slack* (Instant team notification alerts)

---

## ⚙️ Key Features
1. *Dynamic Stage Tracking:* Automatically monitors where a prospect is in the sales funnel and adjusts the ClickUp task status accordingly.
2. *Consolidated Engine:* Combines three independent workflow scenarios into a single, efficient blueprint to optimize system tasks.
3. *Smart Scheduling:* Built-in delays and conditions to prevent spamming leads while keeping communication natural.
