AI-Powered Slack Notifications System
​# Project Overview
​This automation uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze incoming Gmail attachments and send smart, instant alerts to team members on Slack. Instead of just forwarding files, the AI parses the data to provide context, making team collaboration faster and smarter.
​# Tools Used
​Zapier AI: For intelligent data analysis and parsing.
​Gmail: As the trigger source for new business documents.
​Slack: For real-time team communication and alerts.
​# How it Works
​Trigger: A new attachment arrives in Gmail.
​AI Action: Zapier’s AI tool analyzes the content of the document/email to extract key information.
​Outcome: A concise, AI-generated summary or alert is sent directly to the relevant Slack channel, ensuring the team stays informed without opening every email.
​✅ Key Benefits
​Smart Filtering: AI ensures only relevant data is highlighted to the team.
​Speed: Instant notifications reduce response time for critical documents.
​Enhanced Collaboration: Keeps everyone in the loop with automated, high-context alerts.
