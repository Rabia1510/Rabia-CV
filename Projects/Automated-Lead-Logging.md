#  Automated Data Entry & Lead Logging

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on *Revenue Operations (RevOps)* by automating the logging of starred emails and lead inquiries into a central tracking sheet. It transforms a chaotic inbox into a structured database.

##  Tools Used
* *Gmail:* Monitoring starred emails for lead prioritization.
* *Zapier:* Data extraction and transfer.
* *Google Sheets:* Centralized lead tracking and audit log.

## How it Works
1. *Trigger:* An email is "Starred" in Gmail (indicating a high-priority lead or task).
2. *Extraction:* Zapier pulls the Sender Name, Subject Line, and Timestamp.
3. *Outcome:* The information is logged into a "Master Lead Sheet" for real-time tracking and follow-ups.

## ✅ Key Benefits
* *Lead Tracking:* Ensures no high-priority email is forgotten or buried.
* *Workflow Visibility:* Allows managers to see lead volume at a glance.
* *Simplicity:* A low-code solution that significantly improves task management.
